About Me

I like Star Wars

As a “Data Scientist”, I love all things data, statistics, machine learning, and even yes – AI. But I love getting s*** done even more.

I have had the opportunity to consult and work in a wide range of industries: media, financial, industrial, government and non-profit. Everything from a political polling firm to a world-renowned hedge fund. I have consulted on automated predictive modeling, customer segmentation, forecasting, visual analytics, and have even taught training classes.

Don’t let my experience fool you though. Like everyone in our industry, I suffer from inflated expectations, black box mystery, and undeserved respect. I believe Data Science should be digestible, and done in such a way that all walks of an organization can understand and buy in.

A while ago I started my journey by double majoring in Politics and Economics. Like many, I had no idea I would end up in a data-focused field where I would be coding. While working in politics, sports, and operations I realized that my true passion was using data technology to solve problems. I decided to go “all in”, attending the Institute for Advanced Analytics at North Carolina State University to complete my Masters in Analytics.

Since then, I’ve been doing my best to learn everything new, and solve as many fun problems as I can. I hope my experiences will be of benefit to you on your data journey too! Feel free to contact me with any questions or comments.
